About Us

Information about the team

We are a team of software developer apprentices set on a mission to create a project management application. We also have an interest in providing a diverse competitor to help you get your tasks done faster.

Motivation of project

We wanted to be able to provide consumers with a tool that empowers their team to manage any type of project, workflow, or task tracking.Through our training and experiences we were able to produce a product which allows users to create tasks, projects, and issues.
