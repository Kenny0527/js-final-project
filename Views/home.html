To-Do's

Click the corresponding icons to be directed to either the project page, tasks page, calendar page, or recycle bin page.

The projects page will allow you to add projects and click associated tasks within said project. You may view dates for starting and ending dates. You may add, edit, or delete projects on this page

The tasks page will allow you to add projects and click associated tasks within said project. You may view dates for starting and ending dates. You may add, edit, or delete projects on this page

The calendar allow you to view the current month with populated tasks. You will have the ability to click on tasks within the calendar block and manipulate the data.

The recycle bin page show you all the deleted items and give you the ability to restore the deleted tasks. Also, you will be able to permanently delete the items.
